The Artist

"I find beauty in what others overlook — petals past their peak, seeds gone to husk, stems turned to silk."

Timothy Brian is a self-proclaimed artist working from Richmond, Virginia, where he collects, presses, and arranges dried botanicals into framed compositions. Each piece is made entirely by hand using foraged and cultivated plant material — flowers, citrus, leaves, and seed pods preserved at their most expressive.
